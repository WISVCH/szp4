Welcome judge {{ request.user.username }},

The JuryTool is your interface with the contest system. It consists of 5 parts, accessible by the menu buttons on top:

Home

This page, here you can find a small explanation of the five different parts.

Scorelist

These are the actual standings of the contest. Standings are decided by:

  • the total number of problems solved (more is better).
  • the total time used for the solving the problems (less is better).
The time is calculated by the time between the contest start and the time of submission. Every wrong submission costs 20 penalty minutes, but they only count if you eventually solve the problem. They are updated for the jury even if the contest status is set to NOINFO when teams and viewers will see a frozen list.

Clarifications

Here you can read clarification requests from teams and send clarification, global or to a specified team. requests that are not yet answered by any jury member are marked "unanswered".

Submissions

Here you can see all the submissions, possibly filtered on a specified problem. You can see details of the submission and download source code.

Status window

The status window in the upper right corner shows the time since the start of the contest and the contest status.
