the Teamtool is your interface with the contest system. It consists basically of 5 parts, accessible by the menu buttons on top:

Home

This page, here you can find a small explanation of the five different parts.

Scorelist

These are the standings of the contest. They are updated until 1 hour before the end of the contest. Standings are decided by:

  • the total number of problems solved (more is better)
  • the total time used for the solved problems (less is better)
The time is calculated by the time between the contest start and the time of submission. Every wrong submission costs 20 penalty minutes, but they only count if you eventually solve the problem.

Clarifications

Here you can communicate with the jury. You can ask for clarifications on the assignments or on judgements of solutions. The standard answer will be to read the problem specifications better. The jury may send clarifications to you if they find it necessary, if the light "NEW CLARIFICATIONS" is lit, you can find new clarifications in this section. Make sure you read them.

Submissions

Here you can watch the status of your submissions. It is also possible to submit files here, but it's recommended to use the command line tool to submit files

Status window

The status window in the upper right corner notifies you of new results or clarifications and shows the time since the start of the contest. It also shows the current rank of your team.
